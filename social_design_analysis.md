## System read

The social system is intentionally editorial and restrained: cool off-white canvas, paper cards, obsidian contrast moments, sharp borders, tight safe areas, large confident type, and small technical metadata. It should feel closer to a premium product/atelier system than a typical ecommerce social feed.

Core rules:

- Feed posts: `1080x1080`, 80px safe area.
- Stories: `1080x1920`, top 250px and bottom 280px reserved for Instagram UI.
- Reel covers: `1080x1920`, with type centered for `1080x1350` feed crop.
- Minimum type at export: 22pt.
- Wordmark visible on every post.
- One idea per asset.
- No two obsidian posts adjacent in a 9-tile grid.
- Cream/clay stays accent-only; not a dominant background.

## Template library

Square post archetypes:

- `blog-article-post`: paper editorial post; best for education, myths, guides, journal content.
- `single-product-post`: paper card with product image block; best for product spotlights and catalogue education.
- `collection-post`: obsidian/image grid; best for collection/category moments.
- `hero-brand-post`: paper or obsidian with oversized direct headline; best for brand positioning.
- `quote-testimonial-post`: paper card with quote rule; best for testimonials and social proof.
- `sale-promo-post`: obsidian/high-contrast promo; use sparingly.
- `announcement-post`: paper announcement layout; best for launch, location, operational news.

Story archetypes:

- `story-product`: image-led product story with product name and tap CTA.
- `story-quote`: obsidian testimonial/quote story.
- `story-sale`: sparse offer story with discount as the hero.

Carousel patterns:

- `how-it-works`: 5 slides; hook, step 1, step 2, step 3, CTA.
- `before-after`: 3 slides; before paper, process obsidian, after paper.
- `drop-reveal`: 4 slides; tease, detail 1, detail 2, sale.
- `testimonial-set`: 3 quote posts in sequence.

## 180-day calendar read

Campaign span: February 10, 2026 to August 10, 2026.

Structure:

- Phase 1: 30-day daily launch blitz, 30 items.
- Phases 2-6: sustained cadence, 59 planned items in the workbook.
- Total planned feed/reel/carousel items found: 89.
- Recurring stories: weekly schedule for every day of the week.

Content mix found:

- Phase 1: 12 reels, 12 single posts, 6 carousels.
- Phases 2-6: 24 reels, 19 single posts, 16 carousels.
- Static/social-design production workload from workbook: 68 design records after excluding reels and adding recurring stories.

Design workload priority:

1. Build master Canva templates for the 7 square archetypes.
2. Build master Canva templates for the 3 story archetypes.
3. Build carousel slide kits from the square archetypes.
4. Produce Phase 1 static assets first: 12 square posts and 6 carousels.
5. Then batch Phases 2-6 in weekly groups.

## Canva status

Canva is connected. The account currently returns no brand kits through the connector. Brand-template search is blocked by Canva Enterprise requirements, so automated brand-template generation is not available from the connector in this workspace.

Existing Canva designs found:

- `Hair Solutions` — 12 pages, not aligned to the approved social system.
- Multiple older product/social files such as `Lace Hair Systems`, `Instagram Post #3.1 - Hybrid Hair Systems`, and collection banners.

Practical implication: use Canva manually or existing designs as references, but build the approved 2026 social templates from the local design system and the production matrix rather than relying on old Canva files.
