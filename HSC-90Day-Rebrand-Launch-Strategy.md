# Hair Solutions Co. — 90-Day Rebrand Launch Strategy

**Window:** May 11 → August 8, 2026 (90 days inclusive)
**Channels:** Instagram (primary), mirrored to Facebook
**Cadence:** 1 feed post/day × 90 + daily Stories + ~36 Reels woven into the feed
**Positioning shift:** From general e-commerce to *premium editorial men's brand*
**Visual language:** Off-white paper, obsidian black, restrained typography, large breathing room

---

## 1. Strategic intent

This is not just a content calendar. It's a **brand restart** — designed to look, feel, and read like a different company than what existing followers remember. The goal of the 90 days is three things, in order:

1. **Reframe the brand.** Anyone landing on the grid in week 2 should feel they've found a serious men's brand, not an ecommerce store. The first 9 tiles are the new identity, locked.
2. **Re-earn the existing audience.** Old followers who saw heavy-handed promo content should feel they've been invited into something more thoughtful. Educate, don't sell, in the first 21 days.
3. **Build a conversion engine that compounds.** By Day 90, every product page has a matching reel and a matching carousel. Every objection has a piece of content. The grid becomes a sales asset.

### Three principles that govern every post

- **One idea per asset.** No combo posts.
- **The product, the man, or the message — never all three.** A single post is *either* product photography, *or* a man's portrait, *or* a typographic statement. Hybrids feel like ads; singles feel like editorial.
- **Restraint scales.** A 1080×1080 with one sentence beats five bullet points. Whitespace is the brand.

## 2. Brand identity recap (locked from the design system)

Pulled from `Hair Solutions Co - Design System v2.html` and `Hair Solutions Co - Social System.html`:

**Palette**
- `#F4F2ED` Cool off-white — primary canvas
- `#0A0A0A` Obsidian — contrast moments only (no two adjacent in a 9-tile grid)
- `#8A8A85` Stone gray — secondary type, rules
- `#D9D2C5` Clay — accent only, never dominant
- White paper card surface for product moments

**Type**
- Display serif at 64–96pt for hero statements
- Sans at 22pt minimum at export
- Wordmark visible on every post (top-left or top-center)

**Format rules**
- Feed: 1080×1080, 80px safe area on all sides
- Story: 1080×1920, 250px top + 280px bottom reserved for IG UI
- Reel cover: 1080×1920, type centered for 1080×1350 feed crop

**The 9-tile rule**
After the locked opening week, scroll the feed and pull up the most recent 9 tiles. Check:
- No two obsidian (black background) tiles touching
- At least one paper hero per row of three
- One product moment per row of three
- One human/lifestyle moment per row of three

This is the test. Post 9 tiles, scroll up, confirm. If two black tiles are adjacent, swap the next post. Days 1-7 are exempt because the launch arc is locked tile-by-tile.

**Reel rules**
- Always upload native vertical 1080×1920.
- Always upload a separate 1080×1350 cover so the feed crop reads.
- 22pt minimum text on cover.
- Captions on the reel itself are non-negotiable — most viewers watch muted.
- Audio: prefer original or quiet trending. Avoid loud meme audio — off-brand.

## 11. Production workflow (solo founder cadence)

The 90 days only work if production is batched. The rule:

- **Sunday 4–6pm:** Plan the week — confirm captions, schedule via Later/Buffer/Meta Suite.
- **Monday 8–10am:** Shoot the week's product photography (3–4 setups in 2 hours).
- **Wednesday 8–10am:** Film the week's reels (1–2 reels per session).
- **Thursday 6–7pm:** Pull DMs for next Thursday's Q&A.
- **Friday 5–6pm:** Build/refine Canva templates for next week.
- **Saturday morning:** Review the live grid — check 9-tile balance.
